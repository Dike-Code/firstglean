About Us

Who we are

First Glean Concept Company is a healthcare and social enterprise incorporated on November 28, 2013, under the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC2281973). We began full operations in 2015 and have since focused on advancing oral health, providing home support and personal care services, and economically empowering individuals-particularly women and youths. Our flagship product, the Use and Press Tooth-Gum Massager, is approved by NAFDAC (A7-1870L), trademarked, and widely recognized for treating common dental conditions effectively.

We specialize in the production of the Use and Press Tooth-Gum Massager, a unique dental treatment device that massages the gums and addresses up to 89% of dental problems, including halitosis (mouth odour).

Our Mission

To lead the healthcare sector in eradicating dental problems and mouth odour, while expanding access to home support, community healthcare, and personal care services. Our goal includes reducing unemployment by 53% through inclusive economic opportunities for women and youth by 2030.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in dental innovation, home and personal care services, and community health impact-transforming into a multinational company by 2030.
